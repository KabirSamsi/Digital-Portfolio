680 Highway Graffiti




Mediums: Google Docs, Phone Camera


I found this message on the side of the Thornton Exit along the 880 highway. It reads ‘London Relax’ with a couple of other drawings in the image. I think that the intention of this image was vandalism, as it does not seem to have any real artistic intention or deep meaning. At the same time, I think that the way the bubble letters were designed, especially the colors in “London”, gives it a really interesting 3d effect. I would say that it is graffiti, as it is words rather than images, with a lot of emphasis on the bubbling of the letters. Overall, I think that the design is really interesting, and while there may not be any particular thought behind the piece, I find the artwork itself fascinating.
