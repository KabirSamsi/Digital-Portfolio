Memes


First MemeSecond MemeThird Meme




In studying contemporary art, we also touched on memes, which are perhaps the most recent forms of art. Our challenge was to create up to three different memes, relating to our school. They were not supposed to be offensive, but were supposed to be amusing. As I have never really created a proper meme before, I was not sure exactly how to do it, but I came up with a few. It was an interesting process, and I found that creating memes is not nearly as easy as it looks. While I was not good at it at all, I found it very fun.
