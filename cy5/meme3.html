Meme 3




Medium: Google Drawings


This was the third meme I created. It used the famous "Unsettled Tom" template, and with it I talked about how reflections class is supposed to be entirely silent. The idea, as with all unsettled Tom memes, is that a person does an action which seems normal enough, but the next line reveals that their action is actually completely outrageous. That was the idea with this meme - playing loud music is normal, but playing it during a silent time is completely ridiculous. I was mainly mocking reflections for its slightly ridiculous of absolute silence and controlling how we relax. I was also pointing out how other students in class are often goody-two-shoes during class, and so act sanctimonious when a person misbehaves.
