Santa Clara Graffiti




Mediums: Google Docs, Phone Camera


I took this image near Levi’s Stadium in Santa Clara. This image seems to be vandalism, and although the drawing does not look like words, it has qualities of graffiti. However, I do not find the same kind of artistic appeal in this image - while the others showed effort to design the letters in a unique way, this one looks as though the artist randomly spray painted a wall in two minutes and walked off. Even so, it was probably done for a reason, but I don’t particularly like it.
