Graffiti Photographs


Towards the end of Cycle 5, we began focusing a lot on Street Art and Graffiti. We studied artists like Jean-Michel Basquiat and Banksy, and even watched a movie about this called Exit Through the Gift Shop. One of our assignments was to go out and look for examples of graffiti or street art in the streets itself. In the urban areas we visited, this was abundant, and so we were required to take three photos and provide descriptions about each image we found.
