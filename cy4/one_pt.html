One Point Perspective Sketch




Mediums: 0.7 mm Mechanical Pencil, Sketchbook Paper


This was our first assignment concerning drawings by perspective. It was 2-point perspective, and we ended up sketching a variety of things, from pyramids to cubes to block letters, using a horizon line a vanishing point. Although I wasn't very good at it, it was a fun exercise. We also sketched a scene using the same method, which gives the image a thickness, making it appear 2-dimensional. We later began to learn about 2 point perspective, which had a whole new look.
