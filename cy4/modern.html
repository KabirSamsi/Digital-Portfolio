Abstract Expressionism Presentation

Click this link to view the entire presentation.

This presentation was an individual assigment in which we were supposed to choose an art movement from the 20th century. I chose Abstract Expressionism, an era of expressing emotion through abstract, often apparently meaningless artwork, because I found it both fascinating and aesthetically pleasing art. In the process, I learned a lot about its influences, the movements it created, and the famous artists during that time. After researching it, I found it to be a fascinating art period and style.
