Donatello Presentation




Medium: Google Slides

Click this link to view the entire presentation.

This project was done to help us understand more about Renaissance artists. I teamed up with Vibhav and Dmitry to make a presentation about Donatello, an Italian sculptor. It proved to be a fascinating topic, especially since Donatello was an artists whom I had known very little about formerly. We researched things such as his origins, his works, his style and his influences. Along with other groups in our class, we presented our research on Donatello to the class in the form of a slideshow. We also wrote speaking parts which analyzed each sculpture which we showed.
