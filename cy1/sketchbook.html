Sketchbook Cover




Medium: Colored Pencil on Paper



I drew this image on a piece of recycled paper, which I then stapled to my sketchbook. I wanted to put a lot of color into it, so I colored the entire image, while adding things to the image which personalized it. In the center, I drew a hacker. This is because I enjoy studying computers and I thought this would look nice as an image. Around the hacker, I drew several colored lines, which I filled with 0s and 1s. Translated from binary, they are the passcode to my phone. I did this just for fun, and because I thought that it would give it a more genuine and amusing effect.
